What is BIM?

Intelligence Built Into
Every Wall, Floor & Beam

BIM is far more than a 3D drawing. It is a digital process that embeds rich data — materials, costs, schedules, energy ratings, maintenance histories — directly into every element of a building model. At Alpha Geo Solution, we use survey-grade geospatial data as the foundation, ensuring your BIM model is anchored to real-world coordinates from day one.

Full fabrication, assembly, and installation detail. Used by contractors for shop drawings and fabrication.

Workflow & Deliverables

01 — Site Planning & Setup

Scan positions planned to achieve full coverage with sufficient overlap. Control targets placed for registration and absolute positioning.

Multi-scan capture with FARO SCENE software registration — achieving seamless point cloud registration with sub-3mm residuals.

Noise filtering, colour integration, and coordinate system alignment. Density optimisation for downstream BIM and CAD workflows.

04 — Deliverable Production

Final outputs: registered point clouds (RCP/RCS/E57/LAS), 2D floor plans, sections, elevations, Revit models, or 360° virtual tours — as required.
